About Me

Hey! I'm Rishav Shrestha — a passionate student, web developer, and junior tutor based in Katahari, Morang, Nepal. Over the past 4+ years I've been deeply immersed in the world of web development, building everything from static websites to dynamic full-stack applications.

My interests span across full-stack development, graphic design with tools like Canva and Figma, and exploring core CS concepts like Data Structures & Algorithms. I love turning ideas into real, working products — and then teaching others how to do the same.

Currently interning at Passion Chaser as a full-stack developer while also tutoring junior students in programming fundamentals. I believe the best way to learn is to build, and the best way to master is to teach.
